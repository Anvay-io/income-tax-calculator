# 📖 The Story

You've just received a notification...

> **"The Income Tax Department is reviewing your financial records."** 🚔

Don't panic.

Enter your annual income, calculate your estimated tax liability, and make sure you're on the right side of the law before the raid squad reaches your doorstep.

This project transforms a simple income tax calculator into a fun, interactive experience while demonstrating core **JavaScript DOM Manipulation**, **responsive UI design**, and **frontend development**.
